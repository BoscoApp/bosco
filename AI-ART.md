# AI-Generated Artwork Ledger

AI image generation is approved for project art, with guardrails (brief §6). Every AI-generated image
that ships is logged here with its generator, date, and a prompt reference — the same provenance
discipline as any third-party asset in [`CREDITS.md`](CREDITS.md).

## Rules

1. **One locked style spec per asset class.** Off-style outputs are regenerated, not shipped.
2. **Provenance logged** below for every committed AI image: file, generator, date, prompt reference.
3. **Sacred subjects default to the public-domain corpus.** Images of Our Lord, Our Lady, the angels,
   and the saints in devotional contexts come from PD holy cards / missal engravings by default. AI
   generation of sacred imagery requires the owner's **explicit per-image approval**, parallel to the
   doctrinal review gate.
4. **Copyright reality:** purely AI-generated images are not copyrightable under current US doctrine —
   effectively public domain; the project claims no exclusivity over them.
