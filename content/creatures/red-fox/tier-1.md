The red fox looks a little like a small dog. It has red-orange fur, black feet, and a big bushy
tail. The tip of the tail is white.

A fox is very clever. It listens for tiny sounds in the grass. Then it pounces to catch a mouse!

Baby foxes are called kits. They play outside their den in spring. The mother fox brings them food.

Have you ever seen a fox? Look for its bright tail near fields and woods.
