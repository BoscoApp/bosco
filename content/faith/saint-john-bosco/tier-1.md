John Bosco was a boy who loved God. He learned circus tricks to make other children happy. First
he did a trick — then he taught them their prayers!

When he grew up, he became a priest. Everyone called him Don Bosco. He gathered poor boys together
to play, to learn, and to pray. This happy place was called the Oratory.

Don Bosco was never mean. He said kind words work better than hard ones. He helped thousands of
boys become good.

His feast day is January 31. Saint John Bosco, pray for us!
